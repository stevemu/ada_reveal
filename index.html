WCAG (web content accessiblity guidelines)

Perceivable

  • img text alt
  • captions to video
  • color - low contrast - not good

Operable

  • keyboard traps
  • keyboard-only nav
  • media player controls shown
  • enough time to fill out a form
  • hover - won't work with keyboard or touchscreen

Understandable

  • order of page makes sense
  • content clearly written;
  • interaction easy to understand
  • error msg clear and easy to resolve
  • predictable
  • understandable the UI, consistent, not confusing

Robust

  • only support newest browser/os?
  • develoepd with best practices?
  • consider a11y on-going maintenance task
  • can be consumed by a wide range of user agents
